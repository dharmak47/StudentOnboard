# Institute360 Sales SOP

**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Purpose
To acquire new training institutes and coaching centers as paying customers using a standardized sales process.

## 2. Target Customers
**Priority order:**
1. Tuition Centers
2. Coaching Institutes (NEET/JEE)
3. Computer Training Centers
4. Spoken English Institutes
5. Skill Development Centers
6. IAS/Bank Coaching
7. Dance & Music Academies

**Ideal customer profile:**
- 100–1000 students
- 5–50 staff members
- Currently using Excel or manual processes
- Decision maker is the Owner or Principal

## 3. Daily Targets

| New Leads | 30 |

| Emails | 20 |
| Demo Appointments | 5 |
| Live Demos | 2 |
| Follow-up Calls | 10 |
| New Customers | 1 |

## 4. Sales Process
`Lead Generation` ➔ `Cold Call` ➔ `Qualification` ➔ `Book Demo` ➔ `Live Demo` ➔ `Free Trial (30 Days)` ➔ `Follow-up` ➔ `Close Sale` ➔ `Customer Onboarding` ➔ `Referral Request`

### Step 1 – Lead Generation
Collect institute details from:
- Google Maps
- JustDial
- Facebook
- Local directories
- Education exhibitions
- Existing customer referrals

**Record:**
- Institute Name
- Owner Name
- Mobile Number
- Email
- City
- Number of Students
- Current Software

### Step 2 – Cold Calling Script
> "Good morning. Am I speaking with the institute owner? My name is ______ from Institute360. We help coaching centres manage admissions, attendance, fees, parents, staff, and student communication in one platform. May I take just two minutes to explain how institutes are saving time and reducing manual work?"

**If interested:**
> "I'd like to schedule a free 20-minute online demo. Which time works best for you?"

### Step 3 – Qualification Questions
**Ask:**
- How many students do you have?
- How do you collect fees?
- Are you using Excel or software?
- How many branches?
- Biggest challenge?
- Who decides software purchases?

### Step 4 – Demo Preparation
**Before every demo:**
- Know: Student count, Number of branches, Current process, Pain points.
- Prepare demo data.

### Step 5 – Demo Flow (20 Minutes)
1. **Introduction (2 min):** Introduce Institute360.
2. **Problem Discussion (5 min):** Ask about fee collection, attendance, parent communication, reports, admissions.
3. **Product Demo (10 min):** Show Dashboard, Admission, Student Management, Attendance, Fee Collection, WhatsApp Notifications, Reports, Mobile App, AI Features.
4. **Closing (3 min):** Ask: "Would you like to try it free for 30 days?"

### Step 6 – Follow-up Schedule
| Day | Activity |
| :--- | :--- |
| Same Day | Thank You WhatsApp |
| Day 2 | Call |
| Day 5 | Share Success Story |
| Day 7 | Follow-up |
| Day 10 | Final Offer |

*Never stop after only one follow-up.*

### Step 7 – Objection Handling

**"Too expensive"**
> **Reply:** "Our software usually saves several hours of administrative work each week and reduces fee collection delays, making it a worthwhile investment for many institutes."

**"We already use Excel"**
> **Reply:** "Excel is useful for recording data, but Institute360 combines admissions, attendance, fee tracking, reports, and parent communication in one place."

**"Need to discuss with partners"**
> **Reply:** "Absolutely. I can arrange a demo for all decision makers so everyone can evaluate it together."

**"Call later"**
> **Reply:** "Certainly. May I schedule a specific date and time so I don't disturb you unexpectedly?"

### Closing Questions
**Ask:**
- Shall we start the free trial?
- When can we train your staff?
- Who will be the administrator?

## 5. Customer Onboarding
**Within 24 hours:**
- Create account
- Import student data
- Configure fees and classes
- Add staff
- Train users
- Share videos
- Schedule review after one week

## 6. Weekly Sales Review
**Track:**
- Calls Made
- Leads Generated
- Demos Conducted
- Trial Accounts
- Conversions
- Revenue
- Lost Opportunities
- Reasons for Loss
