# UAT Feedback & Proposed Enhancements Document
## Project: Student Onboarding Platform

**Date:** 13 July 2026
**Prepared By:** Malini V
**Status:** Requirements Gathering / UAT Feedback

---

## 📋 Executive Summary
During the recent User Acceptance Testing (UAT) phase, the client reviewed the Student Onboarding Platform and provided feedback for further improvements. This document outlines the 6 new feature requests identified by the client to enhance user experience, administrative control, and tracking capabilities.

---

## 🚀 Proposed Enhancements

### 1. General Enquiry Form (Google Form Style)
**Target User:** Prospective Students / Guests
**Description:** 
A public-facing enquiry form (similar to a Google Form) embedded on the website/app. 
**Key Features:**
* Allows visitors to ask questions or request a callback before signing up.
* Captures Name, Email, Phone Number, and Enquiry Message.
* Admin dashboard gets a new "Enquiries" tab to view and respond to these leads.

### 2. Course Completion Certificate Download
**Target User:** Enrolled Students
**Description:** 
An automated certification generation system for students who successfully finish their enrolled courses.
**Key Features:**
* Automatically unlocks when course progress reaches 100%.
* Generates a branded PDF certificate with the student's name, course name, and completion date.
* Easily downloadable directly from the student's "My Courses" dashboard.

### 3. Payment Receipt Download
**Target User:** Enrolled Students
**Description:** 
A billing and invoicing feature that allows students to download official payment receipts after purchasing a course.
**Key Features:**
* Generates a downloadable PDF receipt containing transaction ID, amount paid, course details, and date.
* Available immediately after successful payment checkout and permanently accessible in the student's "Billing/Payments" history page.

### 4. Course Progress Tracking (e.g., 3-Month Courses)
**Target User:** Enrolled Students & Admins
**Description:** 
A detailed progress tracker to monitor how far a student has progressed through a long-term course (like a 3-month curriculum).
**Key Features:**
* Visual progress bar (0% to 100%) for students on their dashboard.
* Module-by-module tracking (e.g., Month 1, Month 2, Month 3 checkpoints).
* Admins can view individual student progress to identify who is falling behind.

### 5. Admin Analytics & Monthly Reports Page
**Target User:** Administrators
**Description:** 
An advanced analytics page added to the Admin Dashboard to track monthly platform growth and student success rates.
**Key Features:**
* **New Enrollments:** Graph/Count showing how many new students joined the platform this month.
* **Completions:** Data on how many students successfully completed their courses in the current month.
* Date filters to compare current month performance with previous months.

### 6. Admin-to-Student Push Notifications
**Target User:** Administrators
**Description:** 
A communication tool allowing the Admin to broadcast messages or send direct notifications to students.
**Key Features:**
* Admin can type a custom message and send it to all students, a specific cohort (e.g., all React students), or an individual student.
* Notifications appear in a "bell" icon dropdown inside the student's portal.
* (Optional) Can trigger parallel email alerts for urgent announcements.
